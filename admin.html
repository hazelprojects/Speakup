Har lafz ek line mein — format: word | urdu | pronunciation | example

Format: English sentence | Urdu sentence | Reply
Example: How much is this? | یہ کتنے کا ہے؟ | It is 200 rupees.
Format: Rule Title | Formula | Example English | Example Urdu
Har line ek new story line — format: English line | Urdu line
Format: Question | Option1 | Option2 | Option3 | Option4 | CorrectIndex(0-3)
Example: "Buy" ka matlab? | Bechna | Khana | Kharidna | Jana | 2
✅ Dono formats chalte hain:
1️⃣ Gemini format: ALL_DAYS['en'][4] = { ... }; — seedha paste karo
